Global Tariff Database (Beta Version)

Overview

The Global Tariff Database (GTD) contains bilateral statutory tariff rates for 200 importers and their partners, for 34 years (1988–2021), at the HS6-level (HS88/92 Nomenclature). Both applied MFN and preferential tariffs are included, totaling more than 6.9 billion observations.

Note: The GTD does not include other trade policies than MFN and preferential tariffs such as safeguards, anti-dumping tariffs, or tariffs from the US trade war.

Download Data

Pair-level and sector-level tariff data are available for download in .dta and .csv formats, including both simple averages and trade-weighted averages. Sectoral aggregation is provided at the section GTAP and ISIC Rev. 3.3 (2-digit) levels, and separately for agriculture (HS2 ≤ 24) and non-agriculture (HS2 > 24) products. [data]
