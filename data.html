Global Tariff Database (Beta Version v_beta1-2024-12 )

The Global Tariff Database (GTD) provides bilateral statutory tariff rates for 200 importers and their partners, spanning 34 years (1988–2021) at the HS6-level (HS88/92 Nomenclature). It includes both MFN and preferential tariffs, covering over 6.9 billion observations.

The GTD does not include other types of tariffs beyond MFN and preferential tariffs, such as safeguards, anti-dumping tariffs, or tariffs from the US trade war.

Download the Data

Pair-level and sector-level tariff data are available in .dta and .csv formats, including both simple averages and (contemporary) trade-weighted averages.
